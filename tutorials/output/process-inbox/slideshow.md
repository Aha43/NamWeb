# How to process items in the inbox

## desktop

### 1. Everything you capture lands in the Inbox — one place to empty your head.

![Everything you capture lands in the Inbox — one place to empty your head.](desktop/01-inbox-overview.png)

### 2. Pick an item and choose Process to clarify what it really is.

![Pick an item and choose Process to clarify what it really is.](desktop/02-process-open.png)

### 3. Decide its shape — here it is a single next action.

![Decide its shape — here it is a single next action.](desktop/03-clarify-action.png)

### 4. Send it onward and it leaves the Inbox for your Next list, ready to do.

![Send it onward and it leaves the Inbox for your Next list, ready to do.](desktop/04-landed-in-next.png)
